About Me

I'm a Technician Video Game Developer in my final year of studies towards getting my Bachelor's degree at UADE (Universidad de la Empresa Argentina).

I specialize in 2D art, with experience in 3D modeling, texturing, and technical art workflows. With proficiency in Unreal Engine and Unity, I develop gameplay systems using both Blueprints and code (C++ and C#).

My multidisciplinary background gives me a unique perspective, allowing me to bridge the gap between art, programming, and design teams. I have a strong inclination toward product management and cross-disciplinary coordination.
